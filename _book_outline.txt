**Book Title:** The Physiological Blueprint: Modeling Biological Systems with Ordinary Differential Equations in R

**The Persona:** Imagine a 26-year old mentor up-to-date with the latest R packages. He uses phrases like "the architecture of life", "the dance of the nerves", and "the ghost in the machine".
